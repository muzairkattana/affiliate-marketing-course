## üìÖ Day 1-7: Foundation Setup

### ‚úÖ Day 1: Create Daraz Account
**Time**: 15 minutes | **Difficulty**: Easy

1. **Visit**: [Daraz Pakistan](https://www.daraz.pk/)
2. **Click**: "Sign Up" (top-right corner)
3. **Fill**: Email, phone number, strong password
4. **Verify**: Check email for verification link
5. **Complete**: Add profile picture and basic info

**Pro Tip**: Use your real name and Pakistani phone number for payment verification.

### ‚úÖ Day 2: Apply for Affiliate Program
**Time**: 30 minutes | **Difficulty**: Easy

1. **Visit**: [Daraz Affiliate Program](https://www.daraz.pk/affiliate-program)
2. **Click**: "Apply Now" or "Join the Program"
3. **Fill**: Application form with honest information
4. **Provide**: Your marketing channel details (YouTube, Facebook, etc.)
5. **Submit**: Wait 24-48 hours for approval

**What to Include in Application**:
- Channel URLs (even if new)
- Content type you'll create
- Target audience description
- Content creation schedule

### ‚úÖ Day 3-4: Choose Your Niche
**Time**: 2 hours | **Difficulty**: Medium

**High-Demand Niches in Pakistan**:
1. **Electronics** (6% commission)
   - Smartphones under PKR 50,000
   - Laptops for students
   - Accessories and gadgets

2. **Fashion & Apparel** (5% commission)
   - Women's modest wear
   - Traditional Pakistani clothing
   - Affordable fashion for youth

3. **Home Appliances** (8% commission)
   - Small kitchen appliances
   - Air conditioners (summer)
   - Heaters (winter)

4. **Beauty & Personal Care** (6% commission)
   - Skincare for Pakistani climate
   - Men's grooming products
   - Halal cosmetics

**Micro-Niche Examples**:
- "Best smartphones under PKR 30,000 for students"
- "Affordable fashion for Pakistani working women"
- "Kitchen gadgets for joint families"

### ‚úÖ Day 5: Set Up Payment Method
**Time**: 20 minutes | **Difficulty**: Easy

**Options**:
1. **Bank Transfer** (Recommended for larger amounts)
2. **Daraz Wallet** (Fastest, for smaller amounts)
3. **Easypaisa/JazzCash** (Mobile wallet option)

**Documents Needed**:
- CNIC (for verification)
- Bank account details
- Mobile number registered with bank

### ‚úÖ Day 6-7: Research Products
**Time**: 3 hours | **Difficulty**: Medium

**Research Tools**:
- [Google Trends Pakistan](https://trends.google.com/trends/?geo=PK)
- [Daraz Best Sellers](https://www.daraz.pk/best-sellers/)
- Facebook groups in your niche

**What to Research**:
- Product prices and commission rates

## üé¨ Day 22-30: First Content

### ‚úÖ Day 22-24: Research 10 Products
**Time**: 3 hours | **Difficulty**: Medium

**Product Selection Criteria**:
- Commission rate: 4% or higher
- Price range: PKR 2,000-20,000
- Rating: 4 stars or above
- Reviews: 50+ reviews
- Relevance: Fits your niche perfectly

**Create Product Spreadsheet**:
| Product Name | Price | Commission | Rating | Link | Notes |
|-------------|-------|------------|--------|------|-------|
| Example Phone | PKR 25,000 | 6% | 4.5 | [link] | Good for students |

### ‚úÖ Day 25-27: Create 3 YouTube Videos
**Time**: 6 hours | **Difficulty**: Hard

**Video Types**:
1. **Product Review** (10-15 minutes)
   - Unboxing and demonstration
   - Pros and cons
   - Who should buy this

2. **Comparison Video** (8-12 minutes)
   - Product A vs Product B
   - Price comparison
   - Which is better for what

3. **Buying Guide** (12-18 minutes)
   - "Top 5 products under PKR X"
   - Different use cases
   - Final recommendations

**Video Structure Template**:
```
0:00 - Hook (This phone costs PKR 25,000 but...)
0:15 - Introduction and what you'll learn
1:00 - Product overview and specs
3:00 - Hands-on demonstration
6:00 - Pros and cons
8:00 - Comparison with alternatives
9:00 - Who should buy this
10:00 - Price and value
10:30 - Call to action (link in description)
```

### ‚úÖ Day 28-29: Write 5 Blog Posts
**Time**: 4 hours | **Difficulty**: Medium

**Blog Post Ideas**:
1. "Complete Review of [Product Name]"
2. "[Product A] vs [Product B]: Which is Better?"
3. "10 Best [Niche Products] Under PKR 10,000"
4. "How to Choose the Right [Product Type]"
5. "[Product Name]: Honest Review After 30 Days"

**SEO Tips**:
- Include keywords in title and headings
- Write 800+ words for better ranking
- Include product images and videos
- Add affiliate links naturally

### ‚úÖ Day 30: Create 10 TikToks
**Time**: 2 hours | **Difficulty**: Easy

**TikTok Content Ideas**:
1. Quick product unboxing (15-30 seconds)
2. "3 reasons to buy [product]" (20 seconds)

4. "This changed my life" product review (20 seconds)

**TikTok Hooks**:

- "I tested 10 [products] and this one won"
