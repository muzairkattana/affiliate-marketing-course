- **Setting**: Modern, clean home office environment with natural lighting
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, with subjects positioned slightly off-center
- **Depth**: Layered composition with foreground (laptops), mid-ground (people), and background elements

---

### **People & Demographics**
- **Primary Person**: Young Pakistani woman (25-30 years) wearing modern professional attire (hijab optional, culturally appropriate)
- **Secondary Person**: Young Pakistani man (25-30 years) in casual business attire
- **Expression**: Confident, focused, successful, and approachable
- **Pose**: Active working posture - typing, pointing at screen, or reviewing analytics
- **Diversity**: Represent modern Pakistani youth with authentic representation

---

### **Technology & Devices**
- **Primary Device**: Modern laptop showing Daraz affiliate dashboard/analytics

- **Screen Content**: Visible charts, graphs showing growth, commission earnings, and Daraz logo

---

### **Environment & Setting**
- **Location**: Bright, modern home office or co-working space
- **Lighting**: Soft natural light from window side, creating professional atmosphere
- **Background**: Subtle bookshelf with business/marketing books, modern decor

**Final Goal**: Create an inspiring, professional header image that immediately communicates "This is a legitimate, successful path to earning money online through Daraz affiliate marketing in Pakistan" while maintaining cultural authenticity and modern appeal.
